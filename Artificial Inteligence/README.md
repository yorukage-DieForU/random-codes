# Artificial Intelligence

For centuries, humanity has looked at the stars and wondered about creation.

We built tools to understand the world, machines to extend our abilities, and now we are creating systems that can learn from the knowledge we leave behind.

This folder is my journey through Artificial Intelligence: machine learning, neural networks, models, experiments, and everything I discover while trying to understand this new chapter of technology.

But AI is not only about machines.

It reminds us to ask deeper questions.

What makes us human?
What separates knowledge from wisdom?
What is the value of a soul in a world full of artificial minds?

We can create things that imitate intelligence, but only God knows the depth of the human heart.

Some creations will disappear. Some ideas will fail. Some dreams will remain unfinished.

But every attempt teaches us something.

> "Knowledge can be created by humans, but wisdom comes from something greater."

God bless us.
