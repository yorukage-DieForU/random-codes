# Databases

People often forget things.

Names fade, moments disappear, and memories become harder to reach as time passes.

Maybe that is why we created ways to preserve what matters.

This folder is my journey through databases: how information is stored, organized, protected, and retrieved. Here I'll keep everything I learn about SQL, data modeling, queries, and the foundations that keep applications alive.

But databases are not only about storing information.

They remind us that every life is made of countless pieces: memories, choices, people, and moments that shape who we become.

Some things are deleted. Some things are forgotten.

But God remembers everything.

> "What is written in the hearts of people is never truly lost."

God bless us.

The people we miss, the moments we wish we could return to, and the memories we fear losing are all reminders that time is precious. Maybe eternity is the only place where nothing beautiful is ever forgotten."*
